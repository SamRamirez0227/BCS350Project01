About Me!

My name is Rebecca, and I have always loved creating things. Whether it was decorating mugs, designing shirts, or making candles, I enjoyed turning simple items into something unique. What started as a small hobby quickly became something I was truly passionate about. Friends and coworkers would often ask me to create custom gifts for birthdays, holidays, or special occasions.

For me, Texture Studio is more than just a business. It’s a creative space where I can turn ideas into meaningful gifts and help people celebrate important moments with something personal.
